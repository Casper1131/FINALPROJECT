About Vector Services

Our story, mission, and the dedicated team behind your clean and green spaces

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Vector started with a simple mission: to provide exceptional housekeeping and landscaping services that combine professionalism with environmental responsibility.

What began as a small team of 5 dedicated professionals has grown into a company of over 50 trained specialists serving thousands of happy clients across Maryland and beyond.

Our head office is located in Mary Land, and we've been serving customers with integrity and excellence since day one.

Our Mission

To deliver quality service through trained and professional staff who pride themselves on their integrity, making every client's world easy and clean.
