Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

General Questions

How often can I schedule services?
We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and one-time services. You can choose the frequency that works best for your needs and budget.
Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?
Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for all family members, including pets.
Do I need to be home during service?
Not at all. Many clients provide access instructions or a key. We are fully bonded and insured for your peace of mind.

Housekeeping Questions

What's included in a standard clean?
Dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets and floors, mopping hard floors, cleaning kitchen counters and appliances exterior, bathroom cleaning (sinks, toilets, showers), and trash removal.
Do you provide cleaning supplies and equipment?
Yes, we bring all necessary professional-grade supplies and equipment. If you prefer us to use your products, just let us know.

Landscaping Questions

How often should my lawn be mowed?
During growing season (spring and summer), we recommend weekly mowing. In fall and winter, we can switch to bi-weekly or as-needed service.
Do you offer seasonal services?
Absolutely! We provide spring cleanup (leaf removal, debris clearing, bed preparation), summer maintenance, fall leaf removal, and winter pruning services.

Payment & Policies

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Zelle, and cash/check payments.
What is your cancellation policy?
We request 24-hour notice for cancellations. Last-minute cancellations may incur a $25 fee. Weather-related cancellations for landscaping services are always free.

Still have questions? Contact our support team and we'll be happy to help!
